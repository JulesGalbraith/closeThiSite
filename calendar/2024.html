All proceeds from the calendar are sent to a friends' family members seeking refuge in a UN school in Deir al-Balah, Gaza, who plans to distribute funds to others at the school and nearby refugee camp.

This calendar is the effort of many friends and artists with the belief that the revolutions of the past continue to inspire and inform the resistance of the future. The dates marked in this calendar are not exhaustive, but rather an effort of memory and commemoration. There are countless omissions and this reflects on the limits of time and capacity in the compilation, rather than historical priority or importance. If there is a date that we may have missed, please mark it, remember it, honour it, and share it with others.❤ May we collectively be the keepers of our histories.

Many events listed were imperial campaigns that attempted to violently suppress the rights of the people of the global majority. These instances of repression are included with a view to mourning and education, rather than celebration. We invite you to research the events listed each day, to learn more about these events,, to be enraged, and to feel pride and solidarity with these legacies of resistance.
